⚠️ Risk Factors
🌿 Chlorophyll --%
Excess nutrients cause algae to rapidly multiply. As it decays, it sucks all the oxygen out of the water, suffocating fish.
Read the NOAA Fish Impact Report ›
🌊 Turbidity --%
Cloudy water blocks sunlight, killing underwater plants. The suspended dirt directly clogs salmon gills and spreads disease.
Read the USGS Water Science study ›
🥤 Microplastics Proxy --%
Algae grows on floating plastics. Small fish eat the toxic plastic-algae combo, pushing the poison up the food chain to Salmon.
Read the NOAA Marine Debris study ›
